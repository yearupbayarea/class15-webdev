Korean Dramas

I love Korean Dramas! I love watching historical Korean dramas especially because it have action, romance, mystery and comedy. One of my personal favorite historical Korean drama is Dong Yi.

Dong Yi is such an amazing series. The main actress is not like the typical actress in most movies and tv shows who are weak and naive. She endured so much hardships after her father and brother were framed and executed for something that they didn't do. She is such an honest and strong young lady who also have so much knowledge. She does not give up and she will do everything she can to prove her.

Must Watch Dramas!

Secrets is about the wife admitting to a crime that the husband did so that he can keep his job.

Kill Me, Heal Me is about a guy who have personality disorder and a doctor is trying to help him overcome it.

Doctor X is about a lone doctor who can perform surgeries that no one else can.
