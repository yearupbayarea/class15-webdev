Can you guess what it is?

I love going to the gym! I have been trying to get my body back in shape ever since I had my son which is more than 5 years ago. It's really hard to find the time to workout because there's just so much to do when you have a child. They need all of your attention and they drain all of your energy.

When I do find the time to do, I try to work every part of my body. The reason is, who knows when is the next time I'll be able to go back. That's why I use the stairmasters for about 15 - 20 minutes. Then I move on to do some crunches. After about 200 of those, I head over to the machines to workout my arm. When I first started going to the gym, I was not able to lift 20lbs! Can you believe that? I am slowing improving so hopefully I lose a couple of pounds soon.
